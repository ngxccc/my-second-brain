# Establish

## Pronunciation

- **IPA:** /ɪˈstæblɪʃ/
- **Audio:** [Google Translate Pronunciation](https://translate.google.com/?sl=en&tl=vi&text=establish&op=translate)

## Definition

- **Meaning (EN):** to set up, start, or bring into existence (a system, organization, or relationship) on a firm or permanent basis; to prove or show to be true.
- **Meaning (VI):** thiết lập, kiến tạo, thành lập; chứng minh hoặc xác thực một sự thật.
- **Type:** Verb

## Context / Examples

- **IT Context:** "The server failed to **establish** a secure connection to the database server due to a credential mismatch."
- **Daily Context:** "It takes years of consistent high-quality delivery to **establish** a reputation as a trusted vendor."

## Collocations / Word Family

- **Synonyms (Đồng nghĩa):** set up, found, institute, create, prove, verify.
- **Antonyms (Trái nghĩa):** destroy, dismantle, disestablish, abolish.
- **Collocations:**
  - `establish` + a connection (thiết lập kết nối)
  - `establish` + a reputation (xây dựng danh tiếng)
  - `establish` + a relationship (thiết lập mối quan hệ)
