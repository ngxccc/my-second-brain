Distinguish between Throwaway Prototyping and Evolutionary Prototyping.

---

- **Throwaway Prototyping:** A quick prototype built to clarify requirements, which is then discarded. The final system is coded from scratch on a clean architecture.
- **Evolutionary Prototyping:** A prototype built with a scalable architecture, which is iteratively refined and developed directly into the final product.
