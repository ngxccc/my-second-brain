When should you use the Waterfall Model?

---

Waterfall should be used when:

- Project requirements are clear, fixed, and highly unlikely to change.
- Critical systems requiring high safety and precision (e.g., medical, aviation, banking).
- The client wants to define the exact budget, scope, and timeline upfront.
- The technology stack is mature and well-understood by the team.
