What is a "Failure" under ISTQB, and does every Defect lead to a Failure?

---

A Failure is an event in which a component or system deviates from its expected delivery, service, or result. No, a Defect only leads to a Failure if the flawed path is executed. Failures can also be caused by external factors (e.g., environmental conditions, radiation, hardware failure).
